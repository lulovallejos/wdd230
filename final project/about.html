About Us

Bountiful Foods is a small business specializing in locally grown organic foods. We are located in Carlsbad, California, but our food trucks cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.

Bountiful Foods is a small business specializing in locally grown, organic foods.

At Bountiful Foods, we pride ourselves on offering fresh fruits and produce directly from local farmers. In addition, we also work with some national and international suppliers to expand our variety of products.

Owner-managed, our company is dedicated to serving our customers both in our physical store in Carlsbad and in our food trucks. We accept orders over the phone and through our website, providing our customers with convenience and ease in the purchasing and delivery process.

Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic foods, custom beverages and fruit pouches, to both locals and tourists visiting California's beautiful coastal areas. We strive to promote healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience in every aspect of our business.
