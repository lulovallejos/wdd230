Target audience

Our audience is people between 20 and 70 years old, who are just starting their own business as well as for those entrepreneurs with experience in the gastronomic field, mainly in bars and bakeries.

Persona 1

Woman, 25 years old. Single. Lived in CABA all her life. Recently graduated as a pastry chef and has just opened her own business with a friend. She works about 12 hours 6 days a week.

Persona 2

Man, 45 years old. Married with children. Owns a very popular bar in the Belgrano area, CABA for 15 years. He has some employees. He works about 8 hours 5 days a week.

Scenarios

Where can I train my employees?
Where can I get investors for my business?
Where can I get legal advice for my business?
